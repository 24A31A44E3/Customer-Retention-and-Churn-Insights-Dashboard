# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Insights Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI project focuses on analyzing customer churn patterns and identifying key factors that influence customer retention. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and business insights that help organizations understand customer behavior and make data-driven decisions to reduce churn.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer churn trends.
- Identify factors affecting customer retention.
- Compare churn across contract types, internet services, and payment methods.
- Provide actionable insights for improving customer loyalty.

## 📈 Dashboard Metrics
| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Total Customers | 7,043 |
| Churned Customers | 1,869 |
| Retained Customers | 5,174 |
| Churn Rate | 26.54% |

## 📊 Visualizations Included
### 1. Churn Distribution
Shows the percentage of customers who stayed and those who churned.

### 2. Contract Type vs Churn
Analyzes churn behavior across different contract types.

### 3. Internet Service vs Churn
Compares churn rates among various internet service categories.

### 4. Payment Method vs Churn
Identifies payment methods associated with higher churn rates.

### 5. Average Monthly Charges by Churn
Shows the relationship between monthly charges and customer churn.

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Churn Rate is **26.54%**.
- Month-to-Month contract customers exhibit the highest churn.
- Fiber Optic users have a higher tendency to churn.
- Customers using Electronic Check payment methods churn more frequently.
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn.
- Long-term contracts contribute to better customer retention.
